######find all types of supported ciphers by ssh #############
sshd -T | grep "\(ciphers\|macs\)"



#######check if ciphers are supported############
ssh localhost -c arcfour      #arcfour is and example of cipher 


#######configure specific sshd ciphers###########
add to /etc/ssh/sshd_config the following line:

ciphers cipher1,cipher2,etc...
